Dennis Deng

Hi, I’m Dennis, a recent SFU Interactive Arts and Technology graduate with Distinction, focused on UX/UI design and front-end development. With experience in user research, usability testing, interface design, and web/mobile development, I enjoy turning insights into practical, user-centered digital experiences.

Through my co-op at Nutrien and team-based projects, I’ve worked across the product process, from design to front-end implementation, while continuously exploring new tools, software, and AI-powered workflows. I’m open to opportunities in UX/UI design, product design, front-end development, and web/mobile design.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see my journey in designing and developing immersive, user-focused digital experiences.
