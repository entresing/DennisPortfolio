About Me:

Welcome to my website! I’m Dennis Deng, a UI/UX designer and front-end developer focused on blending beauty with practicality. My expertise in Figma, HTML, and CSS allows me to create intuitive digital experiences that seamlessly integrate into everyday life. My goal is to make user interactions feel natural, enriching the experience without adding unnecessary complexity.

I believe in the power of thoughtful design to make technology accessible and engaging, providing users with easy navigation that feels effortless. My recent projects, including "Ratatouille in Concert," have equipped me with both practical skills and a deep understanding of user-centered design. I am passionate about creating digital solutions that not only look great but also serve users’ needs in a meaningful and effective way.

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see my journey in designing and developing immersive, user-focused digital experiences.
