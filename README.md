## What kind of visual language would people hiring for this job expect?
- Aesthetics and Creativity: Especially for UX/UI Designers, showcasing creativity and a good eye for aesthetics in visual designs is crucial. This includes color theory, typography, and the overall visual appeal of designs.
- Design Principles: For UX/UI Designers, a strong grasp of design principles like balance, contrast, hierarchy, and alignment is expected. For Web and Front-end Developers, an understanding of these principles is beneficial, even if not central to their role.
- User-Centric Focus: The ability to visually communicate ideas that prioritize the user experience. This involves creating designs/layouts that are intuitive and user-friendly.
